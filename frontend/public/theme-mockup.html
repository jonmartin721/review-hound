Color study for a sharper, more professional dashboard.

The current black-and-gold palette has a strong personality, but it reads more boutique than analytics. These options keep the layout intact and only shift the visual tone toward trust, clarity, and operational polish.

Recommendation Slate + cobalt is the strongest default. It feels credible, modern, and flexible enough for charts, alerts, and forms without looking generic in a bad way.
What to change with any option Reduce accent usage on every card top border. Keep the accent for actions, active nav, and chart emphasis instead of using it as an all-purpose decoration color.
Why the current palette struggles The warm gold is visually dominant across buttons, nav, links, and badges, so the product tone leans lifestyle or craft brand instead of review intelligence.
